About me

I am a graphic designer turned developer. I am fluent in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery and Bootstrap so I have the technical foundation to create any web designs desired.

I was born and raised in Central Illinois but attended college at Western Carolina University on a full athletic scholarship. There I earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts; Concentration in Graphic Design. I thrived for four years as a Division I student-athlete where I learned the importance of time management, collaborating with various personalities and utilizing determination and discipline to achieve a goal.

As a specialist in print, I desired to become a well-rounded designer so I gained a new skillset from Northwesternâ€™s Coding Boot Camp. The distinguished school supplied accomplished instructors and a top-notch curriculum leaving me with the tools needed to succeed in the world of developing.

My strong work ethic and attention to detail make me a dependable employee. I continue to discover new sources of inspiration to produce fresh, clean layouts that convey a specific message. Think of functional design that utilizes white space to bring focus to what is important. I am a big dreamer whose passion for technology and creativity grows with every new experience.
